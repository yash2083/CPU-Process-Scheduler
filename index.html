CPU Schedulers

Round Robin

The Round Robin algorithm, known for its fairness, allocates each process a fixed time slice, ensuring that all processes get CPU time in a cyclical manner. This approach is simple and efficient, ideal for time-sharing systems.

Round Robin

Priority Scheduling

Priority Scheduling assigns CPU resources based on the priority levels of processes, allowing higher-priority tasks to execute before lower-priority ones. This algorithm is often used where critical tasks need to be completed quickly.
